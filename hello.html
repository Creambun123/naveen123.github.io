**Front-end development** is the art and science of crafting the visible and interactive aspects of websites and web applications. It involves building the user interface, including layout, design, and functionality, using technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Front-end developers ensure that websites are visually appealing, user-friendly, and responsive across different devices. Their work directly impacts the user experience, making it enjoyable and effective.
